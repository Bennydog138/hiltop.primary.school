Admissions

Information for parents on how to apply for Reception and in-year admission.

Reception admissions

Reception applications are managed via the local authority. Applications open in the autumn prior to the school year starting. Please consult your local council website for exact dates and the application form.

Hiltop holds regular open mornings — see our events page for dates. We recommend attending an open morning if you are considering applying.

In-year admissions

To apply for a place during the school year, contact the school office: admissions@hiltop.school or call 01234 567 890. Please provide details of your child's current year group and reasons for the move.

Admissions criteria

  1. Children with an Education, Health and Care Plan naming the school.
  2. Looked-after children and previously looked-after children.
  3. Children living within the school’s catchment area.
  4. Other children, in order of distance from the school.

Full criteria, appeals and in-year application forms are available on our policies page.
