About Hiltop Primary School

We provide a broad, balanced and ambitious curriculum which develops knowledge and skills with curiosity and joy.

Our Vision

To create a learning environment where every child can thrive academically and personally — confident, curious and kind.

Our Values

  • Kindness — we treat each other with respect and care.
  • Resilience — we encourage children to try hard and persevere.
  • Curiosity — we nurture questions and exploration.

Curriculum & Approach

Our curriculum is knowledge-rich, sequenced and inclusive. Assessment is used to support learning, not label children. We emphasise reading and a stepped progression of knowledge in each subject area.

You can download our curriculum policy and other key documents on the policies page.

Community & Partnerships

We work closely with families, local community organisations and feeder secondary schools to ensure smooth transitions and rich learning experiences.
